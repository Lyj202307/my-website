FAQ

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our standard MOQ is 100 PCS per style per colour. For repeat orders or premium blanks, MOQ may vary. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

How long does sampling take?

Standard samples are completed in 7–10 business days. Rush samples (5 days) are available at additional cost. We ship DHL Express worldwide.

What payment terms do you offer?

We accept T/T (wire transfer), L/C at sight, and PayPal for samples. Standard bulk terms are 30% deposit with 70% before shipment.

Can you work from my own tech pack?

Yes. We accept Adobe Illustrator, PDF, or CLO3D tech packs. Our pattern team will review and flag any production concerns within 24 hours.

What certifications do you hold?

We are BSCI audited and work with OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified fabrics. GRS (Global Recycled Standard) and SGS inspection reports are available on request.

Do you offer private label / custom branding?

Absolutely. We handle all branding in-house: woven labels, hang tags, heat-transfer labels, custom packaging, and polybag print. Your brand, our craftsmanship.
